This site can gather the NFT's from your wallet, you pick 3 to fight with. Then you can do the same with someone elses wallet. Have them pick 3 and prepare to fight! My algorithm will convert the rarity of the NFT's you pick into battle stats where they can face off against each other in a game similar to rumble royale!

Don't have a wallet to use? Just hit get random for it to select some random NFT's from the database for you to battle!
Notes: by using this App you are storing versions of your NFT's in our database. This app is currently only set for the ETH blockchain.
